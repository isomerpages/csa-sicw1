The 10th Edition of the SICW took place from 20-23 October 2025, at the Sands Expo and Convention Centre, Singapore, alongside the GovWare Conference and Exhibition.

Under the theme of ‘Shaping the Next Era of Global Cybersecurity’, SICW 2025 drove critical conversations and discussions on the impact of cyber developments on international security and the digital economy, focusing on the shifting global environment and reaffirming the vital role of having an inclusive dialogue in shaping the next era of global cybersecurity.
