#### **2025 Securing Smart Cities**

Securing Smart Cities is a platform that promotes collaboration, the sharing of real-world case studies, and open discussions on unique cyber-physical challenges.

This year, the event focused on the theme: “Cyber-Physical Risks Management in Autonomous Mobile Robots, Humanoids, and Autonomous Vehicles” a timely topic as cities adopt more intelligent systems for mobility, operations, and public services.

Government agencies, industry and technology leaders, and ecosystem partners shared and presented their perspectives on how to better secure these systems and address emerging threats in the Smart City landscape.

The event also featured a panel discussion exploring how different stakeholders can collaborate in cyberspace to strike a balance between convenience, safety, and security.
