**7 October 2021 (Thursday)**

*Format: Hybrid*

This year's SICW will host the inaugural Global Forum on Cyber Expertise (GFCE) South East Asia Regional Meeting. Pegged at the Senior Officials' level, the meeting will bring together the GFCE community and ASEAN stakeholders to identify opportunities and challenges for cyber capacity-building in the region and share good practices and knowledge.

The session will discuss the cyber capacity-building landscape in Southeast Asia as well as collaborative opportunities to enhance coordinated capacity-building efforts in the region.
